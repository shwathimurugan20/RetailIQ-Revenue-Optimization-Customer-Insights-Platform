# RetailIQ-Revenue-Optimization-Customer-Insights-Platform
Built an end-to-end e-commerce analytics solution inspired by Nykaa-style retail platforms using SQL, Python, and Power BI to analyze customer behavior, sales trends, product performance, payment insights, and revenue growth. Designed interactive dashboards for KPI tracking and business intelligence reporting.

# Project Overview

RetailIQ is a complete end-to-end E-Commerce Analytics & Business Intelligence project designed to analyze customer behavior, sales performance, product trends, payment insights, and revenue growth.

The project demonstrates the full analytics workflow including data generation, cleaning, ETL pipeline development, SQL analysis, KPI reporting, and interactive Power BI dashboard creation for business decision-making.

---

# Business Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Track revenue and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Monitor payment and order trends
- Build interactive business dashboards
- Generate actionable business insights
